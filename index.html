Our Leadership Team

Meet the visionary leader driving innovation at AugIx System

NARENDRA SHUKLA

Founder & CEO

Email LinkedIn

Narendra N. Shukla is the Founder & CEO of AugIx Systems, a next-generation venture dedicated to reshaping Governance, Risk and Compliance (GRC) through the integration of emerging technologies. With nearly two decades of international experience across SAP Labs, IBM, and high-growth startups, he brings together technical expertise, strategic foresight, and business leadership to drive meaningful digital transformation.

His journey began at SAP Labs, where he quickly established himself in product development. His innovative contributions earned him the prestigious title of "Development Angel" from the SAP AG Board, recognizing his role in building platforms that continue to power enterprises globally. This early recognition set the tone for a career defined by innovation and impact.

At IBM, Narendra spent over a decade leading critical programs in enterprise architecture, consulting, and project management. Partnering with global clients, he played a key role in guiding large-scale digital transformation initiatives, bringing together technology, process, and strategy to solve complex challenges. His experience here gave him a truly global perspective on enterprise operations and digital governance.

Embracing entrepreneurship, Narendra emerged as an innovation catalyst. He pioneered India's first blockchain-powered experiential ticketing system in collaboration with IRCTC, delivering groundbreaking projects such as Mahakumbh NFT 2025, Aastha 2024, and the Holi Festive NFT Series 2024. Beyond this, his leadership extended to transformative programs with Indian Railways, ONDC, and NISG, where he helped reimagine governance and digital ecosystems for the public and private sectors alike.

Today, with AugIx Systems, Narendra is building a next-generation GRC platform designed to predict risks, automate compliance, and enhance transparency for enterprises and governments. Guided by his philosophy of "Inclusive Smart Digital Governance," he envisions technology as a driver of empowerment, accountability, transparency, and inclusive growth‚Äîreshaping the way organizations and governments operate in the digital age.
